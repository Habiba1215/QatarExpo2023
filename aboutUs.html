ABOUT US

Expo 2023 Doha is set to make history as the first A1 International Horticultural Exhibition in Qatar, the Middle East, and North Africa. Expo 2023 Doha is developed under Qatar's National Vision 2030 organized under the Ministry of Municipality.

MISSION

"To foster innovation, collaboration, and global engagement by
showcasing Qatar's diverse culture, industries, and advancements
at Expo Qatar. We strive to create a platform that promotes
sustainable development, knowledge exchange, and economic
growth, while connecting individuals and businesses from
around the world."

VISION

"To be a world-renowned exposition that embodies Qatar's spirit of progress and inclusivity. We envision Expo Qatar as a catalyst for transformative ideas, forging connections that transcend borders and cultures. By spotlighting Qatar's achievements and its commitment to a sustainable future, we aim to inspire positive change on a global scale."
